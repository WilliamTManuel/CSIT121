Importance

Helpfulness

One of the main reasons geolocation is important is because it makes apps and websites more helpful and personalized. This helpfulness is easily demonstrated whenever we want to check the weather, find a new place to eat nearby, or track our deliveries. For emergencies, on the other hand, geolocation can help responders find people in need or guide them during a crisis. This technology is used in many areas of modern life, from aiding in peoples daily or serious needs, to making software run more smoothly.

Efficiency

Geolocation also saves time and improves accuracy of the websites and apps we use. For example, ride-sharing apps use it to match passengers with drivers, to which an exact exact pickup location and time has to be provided. Delivery apps use geolocation to find the fastest routes, saving time, fuel, and ultimately money. These small improvements in efficiency make everyones lives easier and help businesses serve their customers better.
