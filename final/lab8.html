Description

  • In this lab, I enhanced navigation by adding interactivity to a multipage website.

  • These design choices made my site intuitive, as users could easily access the navigation bar at all times.
  • I applied JavaScript for the first time and it was confusing at first due to the various methods and their order.
  • After understanding it, I used it with other languages to improve my page's interactiveness and accessibility for the user.
