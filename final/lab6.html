Description

  • In this lab, I applied the best web design practices to evaluate the usability of a website.
  • First I looked at how well the content was organized, including its structure and order of sections and topics.
  • Then I seen what CRAP principles were used to determine how well-designed it was in terms of visuals and for users.
  • I also evaluated the website's navigation in terms of how effective and easy to understand it was.
  • Lastly, I determined the accessibility, and if the site provided a pleasant and fair user experience.
