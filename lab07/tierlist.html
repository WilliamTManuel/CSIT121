Reflection

This is a tier list that ranks the most popular gaming consoles in the last few decades, many of which I grew up with. Over the years, gaming consoles have brought me and many others lots of fun and a surplus of fond memories. As these consoles have evolved, so have people's preferences of what console is their favorite; but not by as much as you may think. By categorizing consoles into tiers, I wanted to highlight how technological performance or features is not the only thing that matters. Each console represents a unique chapter in gaming history and they have had a signficant cultural impact and legacy within the gaming community. Some consoles, like the Xbox 360 and PlayStation 2, have left a lasting influence on how games are developed and enjoyed today, and many people find themselves returning to consoles from all eras like a time machine to re-live the past. These experiences are why my favorite console is completely different to someone elses, despite any objective flaws the console may have compared to others.
