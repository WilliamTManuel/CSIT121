Lab 5 - Best Design Practices


There are many factors that go into making a great website. The first design practice that is extremely important, and determines whether or not people will stay on the website, is its user-friendliness. This can be accomplished by making your web page easy to navigate, using a clear layout that allows people to easily get to where they want to go within the site. You also need a clean and aesthetically pleasing page design, which uses easy to read fonts and basic colors. Additionally, in a growing world where websites are viewed on all types of devices, your page should be able to do the same. By using responsive page techniques, you can ensure the contents of your website will work and fit well on the screen of any device, and without error. Overall, applying these design practices to your web page will make it as accessible as possible, provide the user with a pleasant experience, and allow your website to become as successful as it can be.
